This Giro was intense, packed with emotions, unforgettable," said Longo Borghini.</p><p>"I knew I was going to have to give everything and that's what I did. Cycling is full of highs and lows, and moments like this make the sacrifice worthwhile."</p><p>Liane Lippert, also of Movistar, won the extremely hilly 134km eighth and final stage from Forli to Imola.</p><p>The German beat SD Worx-Protime's Anna van der Breggen in a two-up sprint finish.</p><p>Although Reusser got ahead of Longo Borghini for third, the four-second time bonus was not enough
